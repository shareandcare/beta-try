Sharing

Share your online streaming services by listing them on Share & Care to save on monthly streaming subscription costs.

With a few simple clicks, fill out a quick form that asks about your video or music streaming subscription, the plan type and the number of active users.

We’ll review your streaming account, guide you through the next steps and tell you how much you can earn.

Once reviewed and approved, your streaming account is listed on Share & Care, so you can offer streaming content at the best value.

We will consider all video streaming services that you would like to share. Some of the more popular services that get listed and people want are Netflix, Hulu, HBO, Starz and SHOWTIME.

The most popular music streaming services are Spotify, Apple Music & TIDAL.

We will notify you when someone requests to join your shared streaming plan.

Our job is to facilitate the onboarding process and ensure you, the ‘sharer’, properly and securely set up the ‘carer’ on your account.

The maximum number of people allowed on a single streaming account depends on the type of streaming service. Video streaming services have restrictions on the number of simultaneous streams allowed. While certain video streaming services may support more profiles, we believe following the number of simultaneous streams delivers the best viewing experience to our users.

Shared music streaming plans support up to 6 members, including the account owner.

Depending on the type of streaming service, you may be asked to share your password. Video streaming profiles for Netflix and Hulu are gathered under one set of credentials. Music streaming services, like Spotify and TIDAL, allow each member on the plan to have their own account. The individual who started the plan is usually the primary account owner with admin responsibilities.

We suggest that you let us know immediately and provide as much information that you can so we can reach out with the user to discuss the matter. It is advised that you do not directly reach out to any members on your plan.

We are also developing a strike policy to keep everyone honest in order to provide security and comfort amongst all users.

You get paid monthly for all users that Share & Care sets up on any of your streaming subscriptions.

The monthly cost for each user decreases as more people join, with the maximum allowed dependent on the streaming service.

We accept and send money through the following services:
Square Cash (preferred)
Paypal
Venmo
Other

We will set you during the onboarding process.

Our team will reach out and work with you to address any changes or updates to ‘carers’.

Similarly, you are expected to communicate important account changes and updates so we or the ‘carer’ can adjust accordingly.

Aside from that, there is nothing else for you to do except sit back, relax, stream some movies or listen to music as you get rewarded for your kindness!
